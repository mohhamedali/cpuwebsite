About CPU

The purpose of CPU is the support, encouragement, and furtherance of computer science at SUNY Oneonta. We are a forum for students and faculty to share ideas, concepts, technology, information, and insights related to the field of computer science. While we accept a wide range of topics and discussions, we value the quality of meetings over the number of members.

We are also a resource for tutoring and lab assistance (through our officers) for the computer science students of SUNY Oneonta. In addition, we support trips to computer science conferences, technology shows, programming contests, and other related events. Moreover, we encourage and fund speakers in the field to visit our campus and give presentations.

We encourage the design, coding, and promulgation of computer programs, while taking a hard stand against academic dishonesty. Above all, our overriding goal is to educate students in the field of computer science and to have fun while doing it.
