About DevGuideJB

Welcome to DevGuideJB's warm and engaging semester-long mentorship program at SUNY Oneonta! Picture this as your personal invitation to embark on an exciting journey where human connections meet the vast world of computer science. We're not just offering mentorship; we're crafting a shared experience that goes beyond textbooks and code. Imagine having a supportive guide by your side, helping you explore the fascinating realms of software engineering, web development, and more.

Our aim is to make your learning journey feel like a conversation, full of insights, real-world experiences, and the camaraderie that makes learning truly enjoyable. Join us, and let's make your venture into technology not just educational but wonderfully human.

Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program spans 10 weeks, providing a structured and comprehensive experience for students looking to delve into the world of computer science. Whether you are interested in software engineering, web development, or any other field, our mentorship program is tailored to guide you through the fundamentals and real-world applications.
